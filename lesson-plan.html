Lesson Plans & Teaching Resources

Downloadable lesson plans designed for educators, researchers, and community facilitators. Each plan is anchored to a specific film or vignette, providing classroom guides, discussion questions, and activity worksheets rooted in Bhutan's highland heritage.

For educators: All lesson plans are free to download and use for non-commercial educational purposes. Click Preview to read in-browser or Download to save the PDF to your device.
