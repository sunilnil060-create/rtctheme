Small Portraits of a Vast Cultural World

Ethnographic vignettes are short, closely observed accounts of everyday moments — a lamp being lit, a story told over butter tea, a skill passed from elder to child. Together they form a mosaic of highland Bhutanese life: ordinary, sacred, and irreplaceable.
