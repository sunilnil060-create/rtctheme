Explore the Highland Heritage of Bhutan Through Film

These documentary films bring you into the heart of Bhutan's highland communities — into homes, monasteries, and mountain pastures that few outsiders have witnessed. Each film is a collaboration between researchers, storytellers, and the communities themselves, preserving living traditions before they are lost to time.
